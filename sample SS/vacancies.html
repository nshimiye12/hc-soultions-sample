Software Developer

Level: Senior

Job Description

We are seeking a highly skilled and experienced Senior Developer to join our dynamic team. As a Senior Developer, you will play a crucial role in designing, developing, and maintaining software applications that meet the needs of our organization and clients. You will be responsible for leading and mentoring a team of developers, ensuring the successful delivery of high-quality software solutions.

Responsibilities: Lead the development and implementation of software solutions, ensuring adherence to coding standards, best practices, and project requirements. Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product managers, designers, and QA engineers, to gather and analyze requirements, and translate them into technical specifications. Design, develop, and modify software applications using cutting-edge technologies, frameworks, and programming languages. Conduct code reviews and provide constructive feedback to junior developers, promoting code quality and adherence to coding standards. Mentor and guide team members, sharing knowledge and best practices to enhance their technical skills and professional growth. Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies, continuously evaluating and recommending improvements to the development process and tools. Identify and resolve technical issues and bugs,...ensuring the stability and performance of software applications. Collaborate with stakeholders to define project scope, timelines, and deliverables, and provide regular status updates on development progress. Participate in Agile development methodologies, including sprint planning, stand-up meetings, and retrospectives.

Requirements: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field. Proven experience as a Senior Developer or similar role, with a strong track record of successfully delivering complex software projects. In-depth knowledge of programming languages such as Java, C#, Python, or JavaScript, and proficiency in relevant frameworks and libraries. Experience with database design and development, including SQL and NoSQL databases. Strong understanding of software development principles, design patterns, and architectural concepts. Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills, with the ability to think critically and propose innovative solutions. Strong communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to effectively collaborate with team members and stakeholders. Experience with Agile methodologies, such as Scrum or Kanban. Familiarity with version control systems, such as Git. Demonstrated ability to lead and mentor junior developers.

Are you interested? Send us your resume, cover letter and other supporting documents at hc-recruitment@hc.rw
